# Technical Analysis Guide

## Indicator Interpretation

### RSI (Relative Strength Index)
- 0-30: Oversold (potential buy)
- 30-50: Bearish momentum
- 50-70: Bullish momentum
- 70-100: Overbought (potential sell)
- **Divergence**: Price makes new high but RSI doesn't → bearish divergence (strong signal)

### Moving Averages
- Price > SMA20 > SMA50: Strong uptrend
- Price < SMA20 < SMA50: Strong downtrend
- SMA20 crossing SMA50: Trend change signal (golden/death cross)
- Use SMA200 as major support/resistance

### Bollinger Bands
- Band squeeze (narrowing): Volatility compression, breakout imminent
- Walking the band: Strong trend, don't fade it
- Band width > 2σ from mean: Extended, mean reversion likely

## Multi-Timeframe Analysis
1. **Weekly/Daily**: Identify the major trend direction
2. **4H**: Find the setup (pullback to support, pattern completion)
3. **1H/15M**: Fine-tune entry timing

Trade in the direction of the higher timeframe. Counter-trend trades require 2x confirmation.

## Key Levels
- **Support**: Previous lows, round numbers ($60K, $70K), SMA200
- **Resistance**: Previous highs, psychological levels, Fibonacci extensions
- Level tested 3+ times and holding = strong level
- Level tested 3+ times and breaking = strong breakout

## Volume Analysis
- Rising price + rising volume = Healthy trend (continue)
- Rising price + falling volume = Weak rally (caution)
- Falling price + rising volume = Strong selling (exit)
- Falling price + falling volume = Weak selling (may reverse)

## Practical Workflow
1. Check daily trend (SMA20 vs SMA50)
2. Check RSI for extremes
3. Check Bollinger for squeeze/expansion
4. Check volume for confirmation
5. Only trade when 3+ indicators align
